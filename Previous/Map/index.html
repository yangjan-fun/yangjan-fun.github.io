2018 USA Border-Crossing Vehicle Entries

The Bureau of Transportation Statistics (BTS) Border Crossing Data provides summary statistics to the public for inbound crossings at the U.S.-Canada and the U.S.-Mexico border at the port level. Border crossing data are collected at ports of entry by U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP), then compiled and tabulated by staff of CBP's Office of Field Operation. The graph below reflects the number of vehicles entering the United States in 12 months of the year including: buses, personal vehicles, trains, trucks.
